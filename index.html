Frequently Asked Questions

Why Sei?
Fast finality and low fees. Saves confirm in seconds and can be loaded from any tab once confirmed.
What gets saved?
Scene, active layers, and each layer volume in basis points. You have three on-chain slots.
Mic doesn’t work
Allow microphone permissions and start after a click. You can always type the same commands.
Tx stuck or failed
Switch to a healthy Sei testnet RPC in your wallet and retry. Keep a little test SEI for gas.
